How Data Is Used

Our applications are designed to work without collecting personal information. Learning progress, favourites and preferences are stored locally on the user's device whenever possible.

Data Sharing

Sirris Guides does not sell, rent or share personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. Any third-party services integrated into our applications (such as Google Play Services) operate under their own privacy policies.

Security Practices

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to help protect application data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure or destruction. Where applicable, information is stored locally on the user's device rather than on external servers.

Data Deletion

Users can delete locally stored application data at any time by clearing the application's storage or uninstalling the application from their device. If future versions of our applications provide cloud-based accounts, appropriate deletion procedures will be made available.

Google Play Compliance

Sirris Guides applications are developed to comply with Google Play Developer Program Policies, including the User Data Policy and the Data Safety requirements. Whenever new features are introduced, this page will be updated accordingly.
